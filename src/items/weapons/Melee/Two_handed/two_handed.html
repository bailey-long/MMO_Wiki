Two handed weapons

  • Stormbringer - A massive, black-bladed sword that drinks the souls of those it slays and grants its wielder incredible strength.
  • Dragonfire Halberd - A polearm with a blade wreathed in eternal flames, capable of dealing both physical and fire damage.
  • Frostreaver Axe - An enchanted ice axe that freezes enemies upon impact, shattering them with a second strike.
  • Thunder Maul - A colossal hammer that generates thunderous shockwaves with every swing, stunning and damaging foes.
  • Serpentfang Greatsword - A serpent-shaped blade with venomous properties, causing wounds to fester and weaken over time.
  • Celestial Claymore - A sword infused with celestial energy, emitting radiant light and dealing extra damage to evil creatures.
  • Voidscythe - A scythe with a blade forged from the void itself, capable of slicing through reality and creating temporary portals.
  • Drakesong Glaive - A glaive adorned with dragon scales that resonates with draconic power, enhancing the wielder's strength and breath weapon.
  • Phoenix Pike - A polearm that harnesses the rebirth power of the phoenix, allowing the wielder to heal wounds by absorbing fire-based attacks.
  • Starsunder Warhammer - A warhammer embedded with star gems, enabling the wielder to channel cosmic energy for devastating ranged attacks.
