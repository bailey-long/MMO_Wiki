Welcome to MMO wiki!

The player starts from nothing in a large sandbox open world. They will have to fight monsters, craft weapons, trade with npc's and other players to obtain the gear that they require to survive in the world. The game will not have a major plot to it but rather small to large subplots that they can find out about through various quests. For example, a village may have a problem with bandits which the player must fix to gain a reward. The players can build houses, set up stores, host events and in general do mostly whatever they want to. If players want they can capture monsters and pit other players against them in combat for a reward.
