Skills in the game

The level of your skills in the game are one of the most important factors of character growth. You may lose all of your items but if you have good skill levels, you can build back up. Skills are split into two categories, Major and Minor. A major skill is like a category that holds minor skills. The higher level the minor skills in the category are, the higher level the major is.

Major skills are:

  • Attack
  • Capturing
  • Charisma
  • Crafting
  • Defence
  • Fishing
  • Fitness
  • Magic
  • Questing
  • Ranged
  • Riding
  • Strength
  • Taming
  • Terraforming
